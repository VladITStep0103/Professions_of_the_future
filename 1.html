"What is this site about?"

This site is about the professions of the future. The purpose of writing this site is to tell about professions, knowledge of which can potentially help today's youth. This site will list professions that will be relevant in the future.

Note: of course, we do not know what professions will be relevant in the future, however, we can assume, judging by the statistics at the moment (for example: the IT sector is actively developing now, therefore it can be assumed that IT professions will be relevant in the future.)
